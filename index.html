About Me

Mike Miksch is a neophyte software developer, picking up the thread of his interest after having abandoned it more than two decades ago. Prior to this transition, he was a scientist, part-time photographer, questionable father, aspiring failed author, and the occasional inventor of new words. He is also the creator of the comic commentary blog, Superdickery dedicated to advancing the thesis that Superman is, in fact, a dick.

He may, in fact, still be one or more of these things.

Mike resides in Washington state with his wife, daughter, a garage full of robot toys, and entirely too many cats. You can ignore him on Twitter at @MikeMiksch.
